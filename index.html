ANIME
WHAT IS ANIME?
Anime is a Japanese animation style known for its vibrant characters, colorful artwork, and engaging storylines. It spans various genres, including action, romance, fantasy, and science fiction. Anime often adapts from manga, light novels, or video games, and has a global fanbase. Popular series like “Naruto,” “One Piece,” and “Attack on Titan” have contributed to its widespread appeal. Anime conventions and streaming platforms have further boosted its popularity worldwide.
DRAGON BALL
“Dragon Ball” is a Japanese anime series created by Akira Toriyama. It follows the adventures of Goku, a young martial artist with a mysterious past, as he searches for the seven Dragon Balls, which grant any wish when gathered. The series is known for its action-packed battles, unique characters, and humor.

The franchise includes several sequels like “Dragon Ball Z,” “Dragon Ball GT,” and “Dragon Ball Super,” each expanding the universe and introducing new characters and challenges. “Dragon Ball” has become a cultural phenomenon, influencing many other anime and manga
ATTACK ON TITAN
“Attack on Titan” (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a Japanese dark fantasy anime based on Hajime Isayama’s manga. It follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against Titans, giant humanoid creatures that have nearly wiped out humanity. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and shocking plot twists.

Produced by Wit Studio and later by MAPPA, “Attack on Titan” has received critical acclaim and a massive global fanbase. Its themes of survival, freedom, and the human condition resonate deeply with viewers, making it a standout in the anime world
HAIKYU
“Haikyu!!” is a Japanese sports anime based on Haruichi Furudate’s manga. It follows Shoyo Hinata, a determined young volleyball player inspired by a small-statured pro. Despite his height, Hinata forms a team in middle school and faces off against the talented Tobio Kageyama. Their rivalry turns into an unexpected partnership in high school.

The anime, produced by Production I.G, is celebrated for its dynamic animation and compelling character development. It has four seasons and several OVAs, capturing the intense matches and personal growth of the characters
